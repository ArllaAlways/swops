How it works

Register your interest for a swop window. We'll ask for your name, suburb, a bit about your place and what you're after.

What's a swop window?

Rather than matching people year-round, to get started we're running specific weekends where everyone's available at the same time. Kind of like drops for sneakers, but, you know, swops for rental houses. The first is King's Birthday long weekend, 6-8 June 2026.

The match

Our matching engine pairs you with compatible swoppers based on suburb, home type and what you're both looking for. You'll get a brief on each match - their place, their neighbourhood, a bit about who they are.

The call

You choose who you'd like to have a call with. It's a video call - you see their place, they see yours, you figure out if it feels right. No obligation to go ahead.

The swop

If you're both happy, you swop. Same weekend. You're in their place while they're in yours.

What it costs

Swops are free for our first wave of friends. We'll get to money later, but for now we just want to hang out at yours and you at ours.

A note on your lease

Check your lease before you register. Many short-term guest stays are fine - but your lease is your responsibility, not ours.

I'm in

Swops facilitates introductions between renters. It does not create, modify, or take responsibility for any tenancy arrangement.
