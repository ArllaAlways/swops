How it works

Register your interest for a swop window. The first is Inner North ↔ Mornington on Kings Birthday long weekend June 6–8.

The match

We pair you with compatible swoppers based on suburb, home type and what you’re both looking for. You’ll get a brief on each match - broad details about their place, their neighbourhood and a bit about who they are.

The call

You choose who to chat to. It’s a video call - you see their place, they see yours (just like a work meeting where you’re distracted peeping on someone’s dining room). Ask what you need to ask. No obligation to go ahead.

The swop

Same weekend. You’re in their place while they’re in yours.

The vibe

The trust mechanism is the swap itself. You’re in their home while they’re in yours. Neither of you is going anywhere near a bad decision.

What it costs

Free for our first wave of swoppers. We’ll get to money later.

What about my lease?

People ask friends to housesit all the time without calling their landlord. A swop is the same thing - a short stay, no money, someone you’ve met and vetted. The only difference is they’ve got somewhere nice for you too. Leases vary though. Check yours before you register. If you’re unsure, Tenants Victoria and the Tenants’ Union of NSW can help.

What about damage?

You’re staying in someone’s home as a guest. They’re staying in yours. Before your swop both parties sign a simple agreement covering damage responsibility and house rules. We also hold a small protection fund for genuine edge cases.
