How it works

Register your interest for a swop window. The first is Inner North ↔ Mornington on Kings Birthday long weekend June 6–8.

The match

We pair you with compatible swoppers based on suburb, home type and what you’re both looking for. You’ll get a brief on each match - broad details about their place, their neighbourhood and a bit about who they are.

The call

You choose who to call. It’s a video call - you see their place, they see yours. Ask what you need to ask. No obligation to go ahead.

The swop

Same weekend. You’re in their place while they’re in yours.

The vibe

You’ve got enough lords in your life, so we’re staying right out of it. Mutual hospitality where both parties have skin in the game is a much stronger behavioural moderator than reviews. You make your own call about whether it feels right.

What it costs

Free for our first wave of swoppers. We’ll get to money later.

What about my lease?

Many standard leases permit short-term guests (like housesitters). A swop is a reciprocal guest stay - nobody is subletting, nobody is profiting from accommodation and you remain the tenant of your own home throughout. That said leases vary. Check yours before you register. If you’re unsure, Tenants Victoria and the Tenants’ Union of NSW can help.

What about damage?

You’re staying in someone’s home as a guest. They’re staying in yours. Before your swop both parties sign a simple agreement covering damage responsibility and house rules. We also hold a small protection fund for genuine edge cases.
