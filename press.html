Australian renters pay for a holiday house every week. They just can't use it yet.

Melbourne home swapping platform Swops opens registrations to give renters a break, as record rental stress meets an equally stressed international travel market.

In 2023, "cozzie livs" was named Macquarie Dictionary's word of the year. It was a joke, in the way Australian language often processes things that aren't funny at all. Two years on, the numbers have caught up with the punchline. Australian renters are now spending a record 33% of their income on rent, up from a decade average of 29%. Add the surging costs of war-affected international travel and the chances of escaping for a holiday this winter are increasingly slim for the one third of Australians who rent, and even slimmer for the 64% of 20-29 year olds who do.

Swops is a new Melbourne-based platform that matches renters with renters for short breaks in each other's homes. The logic is simple: if you rent, you already pay for a holiday house every week. But that value sits dormant, trapped by a housing system that takes plenty from renters and gives little back. Swops exists to unlock it.

The platform is free to use and no money is exchanged between swoppers. It was founded by a Melbourne renter who got tired of paying rent on an empty house while paying again for somewhere to stay.

The first swop window, Euro Slummer, opens for King's Birthday long weekend, 6-8 June 2026. The pairing is Inner North Melbourne - Brunswick, Fitzroy, Carlton - with the Mornington Peninsula: Mornington, Mt Martha, Rye, Sorrento. City to coast.

Euro Slummer is the tongue-in-cheek answer to a Melbourne 27-year-old having to forego any chance at a 2026 Euro summer. Eat a lukewarm home-delivered lasagne in a Brunswick terrace while your counter-swopper enjoys a winter sunset wearing a puffer jacket atop The Pillars, watching a Google photo memory of themselves in Santorini four summers ago, before cozzie livs was a noun.

Swops is free to use and no money is exchanged between swoppers.

Swops is live at swops.it. Registration for Euro Slummer is open now.
